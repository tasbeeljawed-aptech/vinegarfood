Our Story

A Legacy of
Culinary Mastery

For decades, Vinegar Food Restaurant has stood as the crown jewel of JADONS Ltd., weaving extraordinary culinary experiences into the fabric of our nation's dining culture. Known affectionately as "The Serving Plates," we have cultivated an unparalleled tradition of excellence.

With over 1,000 branches stretching across the country, 72 rotating seasonal menus, and a repertoire of 240,000 meticulously crafted delicacies, we do not merely serve food — we create memories that endure generations.

Every dish is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, authenticity, and the timeless joy of bringing people together around a great meal.
