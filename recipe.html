February 2025 · Chef's Special
Saffron-Infused Biryani Royale

An opulent celebration of aromatic spices, premium basmati rice, and slow-braised tender meat — a recipe passed down through our master chefs for over 40 years.

90 minTotal Time
6Servings
MediumDifficulty
Key Ingredients
  • Premium Basmati Rice – 3 cups
  • Saffron Threads – 1g
  • Whole Spice Mix – 2 tbsp
  • Fried Onions – 1 cup
  • Mint Leaves – 1 bunch
  • Rose Water – 2 tbsp
  • Clarified Butter – 4 tbsp
Method
  1. Soak rice for 30 minutes; parboil with whole spices and salt until 70% cooked.
  2. Marinate protein overnight with yogurt, spices, and caramelised onions.
  3. Layer rice and meat alternately in a heavy-bottomed pot; sprinkle saffron milk.
  4. Seal with dough and cook on dum (slow steam) for 45 minutes on low heat.
  5. Garnish with fried onions, rose water and fresh mint before serving.
